About BookShelf

Built for readers who value organization, reflection and discovery.

Our Mission

BookShelf was created to provide a calm and organized space where readers can manage their personal collections and focus on what truly matters: reading.

What You Can Do

  • Track completed books
  • Manage books currently being read
  • Create a reading wishlist
  • Edit and organize your library
  • Browse your collection through a gallery

Why BookShelf?

Many readers maintain their reading lists across notebooks, spreadsheets and multiple applications. BookShelf brings everything together in a single place with a clean and distraction-free experience.
